Proven system.

EPL 8738 sits in the Northern Central Zone of the Pan-African Damara Orogen — one of the most productive gold and critical minerals corridors in southern Africa. The licence is under 100% Namibian ownership (Simed Trading CC) and holds an Environmental Compliance Certificate (ECC) that permits direct drilling, exploration access, and surface sampling without further permitting delays.

The Otjohorongo Thrust is the primary structural target. It has not been tested by any exploration on this licence. Systematic soil coverage has reached only 8 km² of 393 km² total area. Adjacent licences (EPL 8374, 8234, 8235, held by Blackbird Minerals) have recorded significant geochemical anomalies — suggesting the belt is prospective at regional scale.

A three-phase first-pass surface programme was completed between July 2024 and February 2025 by Damaran Exploration Namibia. All assay results presented on this site are verified from certified raw laboratory data files issued by Genalysis (Perth, Australia).
